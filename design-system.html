& Style The design system is anchored in **Modern Minimalism** with a **Corporate Tech-Forward** edge. It is designed to bridge the gap between high-velocity AI data processing and the grounded, high-stakes world of luxury real estate. The target audience—real estate developers, brokers, and investment analysts—requires a tool that feels authoritative yet effortless to navigate. The aesthetic prioritizes clarity and precision. By utilizing heavy whitespace, a sophisticated dark-leaning neutral palette, and subtle tactile hints, the UI evokes a sense of "calm intelligence." The visual narrative is one of reliability and insight, ensuring that complex AI-driven data feels accessible and actionable rather than overwhelming. ## Colors The color strategy uses a "High-End Professional" palette to establish trust. * **Primary (Emerald Green):** `#064E3B` is used for primary actions and success states, symbolizing growth, stability, and the traditional "green" associated with premium property. * **Secondary (Deep Slate):** `#0F172A` provides the structural backbone, used for sidebars, primary text, and heavy navigation elements to ground the interface. * **Tertiary/Surface (Ice White):** `#F8FAFC` acts as the canvas, distinguishing card backgrounds from the main workspace to prevent visual fatigue. * **Neutral (Slate Gray):** `#64748B` is reserved for secondary information, borders, and icons, maintaining a low-noise environment. Interaction states should follow a strict luminance shift: Hover states for primary elements should deepen the emerald, while secondary buttons should utilize a soft tint of the primary color. ## Typography This design system utilizes **Inter** exclusively to ensure maximum legibility across data-dense dashboards. The type scale is built on a tight 4px baseline grid. For large numeric data (property values, AI confidence scores), use `headline-lg` with a slightly tighter letter-spacing to emphasize the "technical" nature of the tool. Labels for data categories should always use the `label-md` style with uppercase casing to differentiate them from actionable text. On mobile, headlines scale down aggressively to maintain vertical rhythm without forcing excessive scrolling. ## Layout & Spacing The layout follows a **Hybrid Fluid Grid** model. The sidebar remains at a fixed width (280px) to provide a persistent anchor, while the main content area fluidly expands up to a maximum width of 1440px. A strict **8px spacing system** governs all element relationships. * **Desktop:** 12-column grid with 24px gutters. Use large 40px external margins to create a "premium gallery" feel for property cards. * **Tablet:** 8-column grid with 24px margins. Sidebar collapses into a drawer or icon-only rail. * **Mobile:** 4-column grid with 16px margins. Information density should be reduced, prioritizing high-level AI insights over granular data tables. ## Elevation & Depth Visual hierarchy is established through **Ambient Shadows** and **Tonal Layering**. 1. **Floor (Level 0):** The main background (`#F8FAFC`). 2. **Raised (Level 1):** Main content cards and navigation bars use a pure white background with a very soft, diffused shadow (`Y: 2px, Blur: 4px, Color: rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.05)`). 3. **Overlay (Level 2):** Modals, dropdowns, and tooltips use a more pronounced shadow to indicate temporary focus (`Y: 10px, Blur: 20px, Color: rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.1)`). Avoid heavy borders; instead, use 1px strokes in a light neutral (`#E2E8F0`) to define boundaries only when necessary for data separation. ## Shapes The design system employs **Rounded (0.5rem)** corners as the standard. This choice softens the "industrial" feel of data-heavy SaaS tools, making the platform feel more approachable for real estate professionals. * **Standard Elements (Buttons, Inputs, Cards):** 8px (0.5rem) radius. * **Large Containers (Dashboard Sections):** 16px (1rem) radius. * **Search Bars/Chips:** 100px (Pill-shaped) to provide a distinct visual contrast against rectangular data cards. ## Components ### Buttons Primary buttons use the Emerald Green (`#064E3B`) with white text. Secondary buttons should be ghost-style with a Slate Gray border or a soft Slate background. Transitions should be a swift 200ms ease-in-out. ### Input Fields Fields use a solid white background with a 1px border. On focus, the border shifts to the primary green with a 2px outer glow (0.1 opacity) to guide the user's attention. ### Cards (Property & AI Insights) Cards are the primary container. They must feature a padding of 24px (`3 units`). AI-generated insights within cards should be highlighted with a very subtle Emerald tint (`#ECFDF5`) background to distinguish machine-led data from manual entry. ### Data Tables Tables should avoid vertical lines. Use horizontal 1px dividers only. Row hover states should utilize a subtle gray (`#F1F5F9`) to help users track data across wide screens. ### Status Chips Use "semantic" variants of the primary green for "Active" or "Verified" statuses, while using a sophisticated Navy (`#1E293B`) for "Draft" or "Pending" states to maintain the high-end aesthetic.
